Lucky Tells the Story

Lucky Tells the Story: If You Ever Look for Me is a tender meditation on grief, memory, and the love that continues even when time runs out.

Written in poetic reflections and minimalist chapters, this book honors the bond between humans and their pets—the quiet devotion, the ordinary moments that became sacred, and the kind of loss that doesn't ask permission to ache.

It doesn't rush healing.
It doesn't demand closure.

Instead, it holds space for love interrupted by illness, distance, or circumstance—and reminds us that loving deeply, even briefly, was never a mistake.

A gentle book meant to be held, opened slowly, and returned to on hard days.
