About

Some wisdom doesn't speak.

Writer

Timi Kara writes for people who are learning to slow down, unlearn noise, and choose themselves again. What started as private journals has become a small body of work—essays and stories that live here, shared gently. She believes in the quiet work of paying attention and the courage it takes to put it on the page.
